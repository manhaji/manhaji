What the switcher does:
• Sits at the top of every parent tab. Sticky · always one tap from changing the child you're viewing.
• "All children" view aggregates the household — combined balance, combined messages, combined calendar.
• Clicking a child collapses the view to that child only — every tab now scopes to that one student.
• Households with one child still see the switcher but only show that child + a faded "all children" pill that does nothing (or hide it entirely if exactly one child — feedback welcome).
